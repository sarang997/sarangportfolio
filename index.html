About Me

I am currently pursuing an MSc in Statistics and Data Science at the University of Bath. My journey into this field wasn't linear; I started as a Software Engineer, where I spent my time building robust data infrastructure and backend systems. While I enjoyed the engineering challenges, I found myself increasingly drawn to the stories hidden within the data I was handling.

Realizing that my true passion lay in extracting insights and building intelligent systems, I decided to pivot and formalize my knowledge. I am now deeply focused on the theoretical underpinnings of the field, actively studying Statistical Modelling, Probability Theory, and Machine Learning. When I'm not analyzing datasets or studying algorithms, you can usually find me playing Chess. The game's demand for strategic foresight and pattern recognition resonates deeply with my approach to problem-solving in data science.
